How It Works

Upload Proof
Submit a PDF (ID, company doc) to IPFS to apply for creator verification.
02

Other users review your proof and vote. 5 upvotes grants verification.
03

Verified creators set a title, goal in ETH, and deadline.
04
Get Funded
Anyone can contribute ETH. Funds released only if goal is reached.
05
Withdraw or Refund
Creator withdraws on success. Contributors get automatic refunds on failure.
